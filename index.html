Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is DevConf 2026?

The event will take place from October 14-16, 2026 at the San Francisco Convention Center.

Are meals included in the ticket?

Yes, all ticket tiers include breakfast, lunch, and daily coffee breaks. Pro tickets include an additional VIP dinner.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague.

Is there a student discount available?

Absolutely! Please email us at hello@devconf.io with your student ID to receive a 50% discount code.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all main track sessions are recorded. Standard pass holders get 30-day access; Pro holders get lifetime access.

How can I apply to be a volunteer?

Volunteer applications open in June 2026. Keep an eye on our blog and Twitter for the announcement.
